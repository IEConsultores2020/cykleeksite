Our commitment

Cykleek has no doubt that a positive change is possible and firmly believes that All of us can take active part of it. We aim to redefine what is considered waste and facilitate connections between persons, public and private organizations or companies that want to be part of alternatives to traditional linear models of the Throwaway Consumption Culture that goes against natural laws and equally threatens all of us and our Planet Earth as a Unity.

We believe in redefining waste and fostering connections between individuals and organizations to combat the detrimental effects of the Throwaway Consumption Culture.

Join us in closing the loop on waste and preserving our planet for future generations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to minimize or totally avoid the amount of waste reaching landfill or other unsustainable waste management practices, closing loops of materials use or extending their lifespan with different alternatives. More than an app to manage waste Cykleek is also meeting point to join forces and create awareness that solutions, as the problems we deal with, can be similarly massive and effective.

FAQ

How can I get involved?

As consumers of goods, service demanding individuals, groups or manufacturing companies we all use materials, consume food, energy and water. The way this is done -through our habits, choices and lifestyle-can make a great difference on the burdens those actions put on our ecosystems and urban landscapes. If you want to contribute to a Climate-Smart Planet join Cykleek and start managing your waste wisely, trading or giving the opportunity to other users to apply the 7R* and take advantage of stuff you or others want to discard, donate, sell or exchange. Likewise, our App also aims to bring closer users and specialized waste manager service providers for the transport or the adequate treatment/disposal of materials you want to get rid of.

How does it work

Cykleek is a user-friendly App created to assist You with the best options to manage waste and connect with other interested parts to sell, buy, donate, exchange, pay or get paid for the transport, partial or total treatment/disposal of materials generated at your point. Once registered, you can see other users’ locations interested in the materials you want to trade, donate or the services you offer or demand (transport, treatment). It is not meant to replace regular waste collection service providers, just to complement and improve its management. Keep in mind that, as stated in our Terms and Conditions

Why CO2 Emissions?

Cykleek´s promotes best waste management practices that fit your needs and creates opportunities out of what could be considered a loss or burden for the environment. Carbon dioxide Emissions are chosen as indicator due to the critical impact they have on global warming and its direct connection with our life style and daily consumption patterns. When resources use is optimized, water, energy and raw materials are also spared so every time Cykleek is used, a tangible contribution to save resources is achieved too. Regardless the size of the impact, every emission reduced counts and we All can contribute to halt one of the biggest global threats we face nowadays: Climate Change.
