Our commitment

Cykleek has no doubt that a positive change is possible and firmly believes that All of us can take active part of it. We aim to redefine what is considered waste and facilitate connections between persons, public and private organizations or companies that want to be part of alternatives to traditional linear models of the Throwaway Consumption Culture that goes against natural laws and equally threatens all of us and our Planet Earth as a Unity. ...

To influence a true impact, isolated efforts are not sufficient so we are here to accelerate possibilities of closing waste loops where synergies for to a better Planet can spread and grow fast with Your contribution Our VISION is to minimize or totally avoid the amount of waste reaching landfill or other unsustainable waste management practices, preserving materials or extending their lifespan with different alternatives. More than a Platform to manage waste, Cykleek is also meeting point to join forces and create awareness that solutions, as the problems we deal with, can be similarly massive and effective.

How does it work?

Cykleek is a user-friendly platform (website, app) created to assist You with the best options to manage waste and second-hand materials connecting with other interested parts to sell, buy, donate, exchange, pay or get paid for the transport, partial or total treatment/disposal of materials generated at your point. Once registered, you can see other users’ locations interested in the materials you want to trade, donate or the services you offer or demand (transport, treatment). It is not meant to replace regular waste collection service providers, just to complement and enhance waste management, reduce it or totally avoid it. ...

Keep in mind that, as stated in our Terms and Conditions, Cykleek is not responsible for the implications of the agreement made between the parts involved who should naturally follow the local environmental, transport or any other related waste management rules & regulations. After you select the option that best suits your needs and agree with the other part, fill out in the corresponding data -on quantities, type of materials, etc-, the equivalent CO2 Emissions you avoided with your action are automatically generated and accumulated in our green counter. Users who champion avoidance of Emissions will be showcased and rewarded with tailor-made support to further improve their materials consumption and waste management. Homes, Public, private or Community based organizations, any kind of business, shops, factories, restaurants, hotels as well as farms or other rural enterprises are welcome to join and translate eventual losses or impacts of their activities into tangible green opportunities.

Get Involved in four steps

1. Register and agree our Terms and Conditions,

2. Once your Account is created, Log in and announce or search the materials (with specifications) you want to trade or the related services you offer or demand. To increase chances of being contacted it is advised to publish pictures and describe in detail the materials offered.

3. Contact users that have the materials or offer the services of your interest. Choose the best option and close the deal using the transaction method of your preference.

4. Once the operation is completed check out so the emissions avoided by the transaction are registered in our Green Counter.

Why CO2 Emissions?

Cykleek´s promotes best waste management practices that fit your needs and creates opportunities out of what could be considered a loss or burden for the environment. There are different indicators but Carbon dioxide Emissions are chosen due to the critical impact they have on global warming and its direct connection with our life style and daily consumption patterns. When resources use is optimized, water, energy and raw materials are also spared so every time Cykleek is used, it means a tangible contribution to save resources is achieved too. Regardless the size of the impact, every reduced emission counts and we All can contribute to halt one of the biggest global threats we face nowadays: Climate Change.

Anti greenwashing statement

At Cykleek we definitely think that one of the main problems that hinders a real change is to claim delusive achievements announcing that products or processes are sustainable, green, eco, climate smart, neutral or any of the plentiful expressions and terms that saturate media, ads, public opinion and even sectors of the academy. At the same time, it is evident that rising emissions and increasing degradation go hand in hand with those misleading claims, keeping business as usual and suffocating true efforts to stop the devastation. Cykleek decisively commits to combat Greenwashing and will cautiously screen entities that want to join our community and pretend to use it for those dubious purposes.

Short Story of the Rs

Few generations back, concepts like recycling, reusing, reducing or repurposing were far from the public domain in the same sense of today. Despite that, those practices were more observed on a spontaneous way, in accordance to other lifestyles. For the first time in history and since 2007 the Urban Population exceeded the Rural one worldwide. Nowadays the urgency of more sustainable world goes without saying and probably no one disagrees on that, but at the same time complexities in a growing Urban world increase and practicing the Rs is not that straightforward despite the surge of awareness and hype associated....

After a rapid economic growth Japan, being a highly densely populated island nation, quickly faced a problem with their residues and started to think how to overcome that challenge. Twenty years ago (in 2004), Japan´s Prime Minister, Mr Junichiro Koizumi, introduced the 3R (REDUCE, REUSE and RECYCLE) initiative, coinciding with intellectuals, activists and responsible citizens around the world that we have gone too far on an unsustainable Planet. Nine years after, Bea Johnson- in her book Zero Waste Home- complemented the Rs adding REFUSE and ROT (i.e. compost). In the last decade more Rs have been proposed, reaching 7R with RETHINK and REPAIR and even 10 Rs with the introduction of REFURBISH, REPURPOSE AND RECOVER. Others talk about REMANUFACTURE and more synonyms that basically go on to the same direction. At Cykleek All these Rs Can Happen and we are enthusiastic to promote their practice regardless of their names, with special interest on those that AVOID a product or routine consuming resources (“The best waste is the one that is not generated”) like REFUSE (e.g. to stop using Styrofoam to pack lunchboxes!) or RETHINK (e.g. taking PUBLIC TRANSPORT or even better, WALKING or BIKING as possible).

FAQs

Do I have to pay to use Cykleek?

...Cykleek Waste Manager app is free for individuals, green transport companies and organizations taking practical actions for a cleaner Planet. For other users, Cykleek has a basic or premium fee with additional features and discounts for yearly subscribers. Be part of the Change, join like- minded Persons and start Cykleeking for a healthy Planet.

I am interested in buying some recycling materials but I cannot see the contact of users that have those materials, how can I do?

...You can browse among products or specific services related to waste management without being a member (“User”) but to be able to contact and perform transactions you should Sign Up, filling out the corresponding profile so other users can also visualize, contact and offer materials they have or services they demand/offer.

Can my school join Cykleek?

...Schools and other similar institutions are very welcome to join Cykleek like Users as long as they are truly committed to a Climate Smart Planet and their corresponding efforts translated in reduction of emissions are checked out in our green counter (e.g. from recycling, reusing, etc). Those reaching outstanding figures will have recognition and can be upgraded to EKO member. Awareness or cleaning up campaigns taken by institutions will also be highlighted and announced in our Platform.

I have an old couch in good condition that can be easily upholstered and I want to donate it, can I use Cykleek for that?

... Yes, apart from selling or paying someone to REPAIR it, you definitely can choose DONATE as one of the options once you are registered. You can also find a registered TRASPORTER close to you in case you want to dispose furniture in a nearby recycling station also displayed in our app.

In my grocery store sometimes there are products with a short BB (Best Before) date that I want to sell out at a lower price, can I use Cykleek to announce such discounts?

... Yes, this can be a good example of the many ways to create opportunities from what could be considered a loss (economic, unwanted waste). You can subscribe in the Business Category for a monthly fee and eventually get feedback insights from Cykleek on the type of items that repeatedly end up in the shelves with a short BB date. To do that, keep in mind to input the characteristics of those products, quantities, brands, etc in the corresponding description fields of our app.

I have plenty of newspapers in my basement and need carton boxes, can I use Cykleek to exchange those items?

... One of the options of Users is EXCHANGE; after registration Users can visualize other Users that also want to EXCHANGE and contact the most convenient to agree on the conditions and characteristics of the materials. Once again, it is recommended to publish details on those materials so other users get interested and can also contact you.

Do I have to pay transactions using the platforms indicated in Cykleek?

... The payment platforms specified in for the transactions between Users are only some of the most common paying methods in places where Cykleek works but the method chosen is discretional of the parties involved. Cykleek does not promote any specific Payment Platform and when transactions are done through these platforms Users naturally follow their Terms and Conditions.

I have around 20 litres of Paint from different colors that are not useful anymore (dry, compacted), can I pay someone to take these materials?

... One of the options given for Users in Cykleek is to find Disposal/Specialized services for some materials that may not be reusable or recyclable in any way. In the case of paint some of them can be considered Hazardous Waste and have to be properly disposed. Remember that as stated in our Terms and Conditions, Cykleek has no control of such processes and You and the service provider are responsible to follow the environmental Rules & Regulations for managing, transforming or disposing those materials.
